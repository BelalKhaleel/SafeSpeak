Know the Signs

What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is bullying that happens online or through digital devices — phones, computers, and tablets. It can happen anywhere, anytime, and to anyone.

💬

Harassment

Repeated hurtful, threatening, or demeaning messages sent to or about someone online.

😶

Exclusion

Intentionally leaving someone out of online groups, chats, or conversations to isolate them.

🎭

Impersonation

Creating fake accounts or pretending to be someone else to damage their reputation.

📸

Sharing Without Consent

Posting private images, messages, or information without the person's permission.

🔥

Flaming

Sending hostile or offensive messages to provoke or anger someone in online spaces.

🧟

Cyberstalking

Repeatedly contacting or monitoring someone online in a way that causes fear or distress.
